Description

Floor Kids is a touch dance action game for iOS and Android platforms that challenges the rhythmic, competitive, and freestyle skills of players who must master the moves of hand-drawn animated Bboys and Bgirls. Competing to original tunes by Kid Koala, players must recruit dancers to their crew, unlock locations, and earn the recognition of fellow breakers. Floor Kids celebrates self-xpression and diversity through the portrayal of street dance culture in super cute, super confident style.

Features

  • Original music to battle to by Kid Koala;
  • Original artwork and animation by Jon Ng;
  • Fresh mix of structured rhythm game + combat mechanic elements allowing for expressive freestyle moves;
  • Gesture recognition system modeled after dance motions, so players FEEL like their fingers are dancing;
  • Video replay sharing so players can show off their moves and choreographies;
  • Storyline underscoring importance of diversity, self-expression, and friendship;
