About the Game

 

Floor Kids is a unique musical dance action game for mobile devices, where players guide a group of young breakers as they participate in dance battles across their city. Featuring challenging skills-based gameplay, engaging characters and storylines, as well as competitive multiplayer mode and video replay sharing functionality, Floor Kids offers players a new game experience based on street dance culture.
